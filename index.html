Who Are We ?

IEEE-CBIT is a student-run platform to foster technological innovations and nourish excellence in the rising technical community. This renowned student branch of IEEE consists of three societies namely, Power and Energy Society (PES), Computer Society (CS), Signal Processing Society (SPS), and an affinity group, Women In Engineering (WIE). Its also praised for being an awardee of the prestigious ‘Upcoming Student Branch award’ in the Hyderabad Section Student Congress held in December 2017.

The student organization, which works under the supervision of the student executive board formed annually in college, deals with the internal affairs and the operations of the group. This establishment is especially known for its transparency and its inclusive nature regarding decision making and moving forward. There are several departments under this club that are structured cohesively for the smooth operating of all the activities under its banner.

Established in 1993, this student branch offers a wide range of learning and career enhancement opportunities within engineering, science, and research to its members and fellow students. Being one of the most active branches in Hyderabad, IEEE-CBIT strives to fulfill the vision and mission of the IEEE global community for the benefit of humanity. So far it has been successful in helping students shape their careers by doing research and paper publishing on a global scale. Vision, Mission, and exhibition are the three pillars of IEEE-CBIT firmly embedded on its foundation.

PRINCIPAL CBIT

IEEE is dedicated towards advancing technological innovation, excellence and is directed towards the advancement of the theory and practice of students. The IEEE provides a wide spectrum of opportunities in multiple field such as engineering sciences, research, and technology. The goal of IEEE education programs is to ensure the enrichnment of knowledge and development of skills in the electricity-related technical professions and to foster individual commitment to continuing education among IEEE members, the engineering and scientific communities, and the general public.



CSE HOD CBIT

I am happy to introduce my-self as advisor for COMPUTER SOCIETY in IEEE CBIT student branch. It is a common presumption that the term “IEEE” is only related to electronics and hence is not of use to a student who is in the field of Computer Science but nothing can be farther from the truth.It can be said that if electronics form the body of a system, it is coding that forms the soul of that system.Hence one can immediately infer the interdependence of these two fields of Science and in turn understand the essential role that IEEE plays in the futher integration and development of these fields.



ECE HOD CBIT

IEEE is a word that has become entirely synonymous with technical innovation and development and rightly so. It is an international organization which is dedicated to provide a platform for students, professionals ,scientists and pedagogues to showcase their talent, innovations,address some very confusing dilemmas which come up during research,search for funding and what not. The research papers published in its journals are quite illuminating and are a rich source of knowledge. Ours is a competitive world where entrepreneurs,CEOs are searching for individuals with a diverse knowledge base and also other talents such as time management , leadership,determination etc.



EEE HOD CBIT

IEEE, being internationally spread, adds a huge value to all of its members by providing various aids and services. Every member is informed about the national/global activities(seminars, contests and conferences) that are held by IEEE International. Regular email updates on technology, monthly magazines with various incentives in global sciences are delivered to the students doorstep. The various competitions that IEEE conducts gives students a platform to expose their talents and also encourages them by granting sponsorships for the execution and enhancement of those projects. Furthermore, there would be opportunities to become a member of specific technology groups such as IEEE Power and Energy Society, IEEE Robotics and Automation society and students can participate in those activities.

Visit To Srisailam 

             	    	
Event title:Visit to Srisailam.
Event category:IEEE PES 
Date:IEEE PES .
Objective:The purpose of this Industrial visit was to provide an excellent opportunity to all the students & faculties, to interact and know more about industrial environment. The intention was to help the students to create a practical perspective on the World of Engineering.
Event Description:The Srisailam Dam is a dam constructed across the Krishna River at Srisailam in the Kurnool district in the state of Andhra Pradesh in India and is the 3rd largest capacity hydroelectric project in the country.
The dam was constructed in a deep gorge in the Nallamala Hills in Kurnool district, 300 m (980 ft) above sea level. It is 512 m (1,680 ft) long, 269.748 m (885.00 ft) high and has 12 radial crest gates. It has a reservoir of 800 km2 (310 sq mi). The left bank power station houses 6 × 150 MW   reversible Francis-pump turbines (for pumped-storage) and the right bank contains 7 × 110 MW Francis-turbine generators.
The power generated is around 150MW these generators are connected in parallel. These individual generators are connected to transformers of rating 190MVA. These transformers step up the voltage from 13.8kv to 400kv. These secondary windings of transformer which are at 400kv are connected to a switch yard with the help of underground XLPE cables. 
In switch yard CTs and PTs are connected to monitor the currents, voltages, power factors and load
